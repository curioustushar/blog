Ratio definitions

Xi = tokens when encoding language corpus ÷ faithful units for that language

A faithful unit is one Unicode letter/mark/number run, or one visible punctuation/symbol character.

  • X1 English
  • X2 Hindi
  • X3 Telugu
  • X4 Tamil

All languages share one 10k vocab. Tokenizers must pass decode(encode(text)) on visible characters (e.g. India's population is 1,428,627,663.).
